Sample size webpages

2018/01/03

There are very strong proponents for sample size calculation in research proposal. And also, there are various approaches to calculate sample size. For example, to calculate the sample size in a study that wants

  1. to estimate the prevalence
  2. to compare the mean difference between 2 treatments or groups
  3. to estimate the correlation between 2 numerical variables

Previously, I showed and calculated for my clients/students but recently, I have changed my style. I navigated them to various websites that offer sample size calculator. I teach them the concepts and I encourage them to calculate the sample size using those calculators. If they need to verify the results, they can always come back to me â€¦..

So, for the specific objectives list above, the corresponding webpage is listed

  1. http://epitools.ausvet.com.au/content.php?page=1Proportion
  2. http://www.openepi.com/SampleSize/SSMean.htm
  3. https://www2.ccrb.cuhk.edu.hk/stat/other/correlation.htm
